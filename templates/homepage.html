About Haoran

I have been working for Amazon as a software engineer since March 2014. Together with great engineers, I design and build large scale systems for Amazon Video that serve over twenty million request per second. Building those powerful services make me happy, and I am proud of making Amazon Video one of the best online video streaming service. I grow fast from and with the team, by learning from those brilliant engineers, and by contributing back to the team. Late in 2015, I got promoted. I am still learning a great deal of things from this tech giant. And with the knowledge I gained, I keep solving more interesting and challenging problems.

Technically, my career started while I was pursuing my master degree in the University of Arizona in Tucson, AZ. I got a part-time on-campus job working as a full-stack web application engineer. With two experienced mentors, I helped building websites for two government projects for the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences. I appreciated the wonderful year working there. It is the exact experience that prepared me with industrial experience. When I graduated from the Eller College of Management with a Master Degree in Management Information System, I also achieved a lot in that part-time job and harvested valuable set of skills and experience.
